Based on the Microsoft Digital Defense Report 2023, the top 10 significant security threats faced by Microsoft are:
1. **Successful Identity Attacks**: These include brute-force, password spray, and adversary-in-the-middle (AiTM) attacks targeting identity systems.
2. **Ransomware**: Human-operated ransomware has grown substantially, often involving remote encryption and data exfiltration.
3. **Targeted Phishing**: Phishing campaigns leveraging malware or AiTM techniques to steal credentials and compromise users.
4. **Business Email Compromise (BEC)**: Methods such as email hijacking and spamming with malicious applications for financial fraud.
5. **Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) Attacks**: These attacks have become more scalable and damaging, often utilizing cloud computing resources.
6. **Data Exfiltration**: Attackers increasingly steal sensitive data, sometimes without deploying ransomware.
7. **Infostealers**: Malware designed to extract browser-stored credentials and sensitive enterprise data.
8. **Cryptojacking**: Unauthorized use of cloud resources to mine cryptocurrency.
9. **Nation-State Threats**: Aggressive cyber espionage and destructive malware deployment by actors from countries such as Russia, China, and Iran.
10. **Cyber Mercenaries**: Private actors offering advanced cyber capabilities and tools to customers for offensive operations.
